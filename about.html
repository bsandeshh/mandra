Twenty three years ago, as a visionary leader and after a brief political career, Dr. Sidheshwar Bellale, along with other distinguished leaders from various fields across the Marathwada region‚Äî Dr. Bapurao Dhage, Prof. Rajkumar Kawade, Dr. Dillip Palimkar, Dr. Bhalchandra Karande, Sou. Jyoti Kadwade, Prof. Ram Bellale, Prof. Bapurao Shinde, and Sou. Meenabai Khedkar‚Äî recognized the need for higher education and research to drive regional progress and prosperity.

United by a shared vision, these distinguished individuals established a research hub and a mathematics institution in Ahmedpur and other parts of Marathwada. Inspired by their admiration for mathematics, they named the institute Balaghat Mathematical Sciences Development Society (BMSD Society).

The journey began in 1999 with the establishment of Bellale's Mathematics Institution in Ahmedpur. Over time, the society expanded rapidly, setting up CBSE and ICSE schools, junior colleges, research hubs, and mathematics institutions to cater to the evolving educational needs of the region.

‚óÜ‚óá Manjara International School (MIS) ‚óá‚óÜ
In 2018-2019, MIS was founded by UDISE : 27280101009 under the aegis of BMSD Society, established by Hon' Dr. Sidheshwar Bellale. MIS is a premier educational institution known for its holistic approach to learning, modern infrastructure, and serene environment, making it an ideal place for students to grow.

Away from the distractions of city life and unnecessary violence, the school offers a clean, natural, and energetic atmosphere where children can thrive. State-of-the-art amenities further enhance the learning experience, ensuring that students feel engaged and comfortable.

MIS focuses on continuous and comprehensive education, fostering students' intellectual, social, emotional, and physical development. The school nurtures logical thinking, social awareness, and research-oriented learning, shaping well-rounded individuals prepared for the future.

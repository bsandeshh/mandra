School Facilities

At MIS, every child should rise and reach excellence. That's why we provide world-class facilities designed to enhance learning, safety, and overall student well-being, ensuring that every child has the resources and opportunities to rise towards excellence.

Academic Facilities

  • Smart board-equipped classrooms
  • Well-stocked library with digital resources
  • Fully equipped science, computer & language labs
  • AV room for interactive learning

Administrative & Support

  • Principal & staff rooms with modern amenities
  • Private counseling room for student guidance
  • School in-take of 250 students

Sports & Recreation

  • Environment Friendly Playgrounds
  • Indoor sports complex (yoga, chess)
  • Outdoor fields (football, cricket, track)

Health & Safety

  • Medical room with trained nurse
  • Fire safety measures in place
  • 24/7 CCTV surveillance for security

Technology & Digital Infra

  • High-speed Wi-Fi & internet
  • Smart classrooms with projectors
  • School management software for attendance & fees

Transport & Accessibility

  • GPS-tracked school buses
  • Properly maintained vehicles
  • Disabled-friendly ramps & elevators

Environmental Sustainability

  • Green campus with trees & gardens
  • Solar power
  • Segregated waste management system
